MANIFEST

After being presumed dead, 191 passengers of the Montego Air Flight 828 try reintegrating into society. However, disturbing realities come to light, which unsettles them.

The series was added to Netflix shortly before it was canceled and immediately topped the viewing charts, persuading Warner Bros to renew Manifest for a fourth and final season consisting of twenty episodes

PLOT-As they rejoin society, the passengers begin to face the fact that their lives and loved ones are not the same as they were, while they also begin to experience guiding voices and visions representing events yet to occur, referred to as 'callings.'
